![](images/ed99a2582d48244f476897b3bfdd6586_991x911.png)

![](images/991476b7664c0e4ab07a7b8a39b549bf_1616x289.png)
3.在【demo表测试表单权限】下配置权限菜单
![](images/23265e62bacf7ab021ff9b61d9656e95_824x591.png)
4. 进入角色管理，进行角色授权
![](images/523e82d0e43a9f15750e336892860b06_791x630.png)
